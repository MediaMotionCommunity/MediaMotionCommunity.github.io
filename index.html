A new 3D interface to browse your files differently.

No mouse needed.

Mediamotion presents a new way of controlling your computer that does not rely on a mouse, a keyboard, or a touchscreen. Explore your files simply with hand gestures.

Play all your medias

In addition to exploring your files, you can also read the built-in file types with Mediamotion. Images, Musics, Videos and PDFs, are all included by default. The different players can be controlled with movement detection just like the file explorer.

An interface built for motion detection.

Mediamotion is not a simple file explorer with a motion detection gimmick. The interface is made to reflect the new possibilities offered by motion detection. It features a 3D view in order to take into account every direction a hand movement can take.

In the same way touchscreen devices use big tiles to ease screen taps, Mediamotion displays several UI elements that can be accessed with simple gestures.

Easy: just click the download link and save the project to your computer. Mediamotion is available for Windows and OSX. Once you downloaded it, you just have to run it with your Leap Motion Controller plugged-in!
